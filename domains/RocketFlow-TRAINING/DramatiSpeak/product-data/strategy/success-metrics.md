# Success Metrics & OKRs: DramatiSpeak™

## North Star Metric

### Daily Active Translations (DAT)

**Definition:** Average number of dramatic moments successfully translated per active device per day

**Why This Metric:**
* Directly measures core product value (drama detection & translation)
* Leading indicator of engagement and retention
* Correlates with user satisfaction and word-of-mouth growth
* Increases over time as ML models improve personalization

**Target:** 4.5 DAT by Q2 2026

**Current Baseline:** 3.2 DAT (pilot data)

**How to Move It:**
* Improve ODE accuracy (more moments detected)
* Optimize notification timing (users engage more)
* Expand drama categories (capture more moment types)
* Enhance pet personality profiling (better personalization)

## Metric Definitions & Measurement

### Engagement Metrics

#### Daily Active Users (DAU)
* **Definition:** Unique users who opened the app or received a translation notification on a given day
* **Measurement:** Firebase Analytics event tracking
* **Target:** 65% of activated devices (97,500 DAU at 150K devices)

#### Daily Active Translations (DAT)
* **Definition:** Average number of dramatic moments translated per active device per day
* **Measurement:** Backend log aggregation (translation_events table)
* **Calculation:** SUM(translation_events) / COUNT(active_devices) / days_in_period
* **Target:** 4.5 by Q2 2026

#### Translation Engagement Rate
* **Definition:** % of translation notifications opened/viewed by user
* **Measurement:** (notification_opens / notification_sends) * 100
* **Target:** 70%

---

### Retention Metrics

#### D7 Retention
* **Definition:** % of users active on day 7 after device activation
* **Measurement:** Cohort analysis via Amplitude
* **Target:** 75%

#### D30 Retention
* **Definition:** % of users active on day 30 after device activation
* **Measurement:** Cohort analysis via Amplitude
* **Target:** 60%

#### D90 Retention
* **Definition:** % of users active on day 90 after device activation
* **Measurement:** Cohort analysis via Amplitude
* **Target:** 45%

#### Monthly Churn Rate
* **Definition:** % of active users (previous month) who become inactive (current month)
* **Measurement:** ((active_users_prev_month - active_users_current_month) / active_users_prev_month) * 100
* **Target:** <15%

---

### Monetization Metrics

#### Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
* **Definition:** Total revenue / total active users
* **Measurement:** Stripe revenue data / active device count
* **Calculation:** (device_revenue + subscription_revenue) / active_users
* **Target:** $178 (Year 1)

#### Subscription Attach Rate
* **Definition:** % of active users with paid subscription
* **Measurement:** COUNT(active_subscribers) / COUNT(active_users) * 100
* **Target:** 20% (Q2), 25% (Q4)

#### Lifetime Value (LTV)
* **Definition:** Predicted revenue per customer over 3-year lifetime
* **Measurement:** (ARPU * avg_lifetime_months * gross_margin)
* **Calculation:** ($178 ARPU * 18 months * 0.55 margin) = $176 LTV
* **Target:** $215 (includes referral value)

#### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
* **Definition:** Total acquisition spend / new customers acquired
* **Measurement:** (marketing_spend + sales_spend) / new_customers
* **Target:** $45 (blended), $38 (Q4 after viral growth)

#### LTV:CAC Ratio
* **Definition:** Ratio of lifetime value to acquisition cost
* **Measurement:** LTV / CAC
* **Target:** 4.5:1 (healthy SaaS benchmark is 3:1+)

---

### Virality Metrics

#### Viral Coefficient (K-Factor)
* **Definition:** Number of new users generated per existing user
* **Measurement:** (invites_sent * conversion_rate) per user
* **Calculation:** (total_referral_signups / total_existing_users)
* **Target:** 0.35 (sustainable viral growth)

#### Share Rate
* **Definition:** % of translations shared to social media
* **Measurement:** (share_events / translation_events) * 100
* **Target:** 40% weekly share rate

#### Referral Conversion Rate
* **Definition:** % of referred users who purchase
* **Measurement:** (referral_purchases / referral_clicks) * 100
* **Target:** 12%

---

### Product Quality Metrics

#### ML Accuracy Score
* **Definition:** % of drama detections correctly classified (validated by user feedback)
* **Measurement:** User thumbs-up/down feedback on translations
* **Calculation:** (thumbs_up / (thumbs_up + thumbs_down)) * 100
* **Target:** 85%

#### Net Promoter Score (NPS)
* **Definition:** Likelihood to recommend (0-10 scale)
* **Measurement:** In-app survey (quarterly)
* **Calculation:** % Promoters (9-10) - % Detractors (0-6)
* **Target:** 55+

#### Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
* **Definition:** Satisfaction with product experience (1-5 scale)
* **Measurement:** Post-interaction surveys, app store ratings
* **Target:** 4.5/5.0

#### App Crash Rate
* **Definition:** % of app sessions ending in crash
* **Measurement:** Firebase Crashlytics
* **Target:** <0.5%

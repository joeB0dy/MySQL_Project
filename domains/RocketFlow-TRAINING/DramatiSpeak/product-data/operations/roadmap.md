# Product Roadmap: DramatiSpeak™

**Last Updated:** December 19, 2025
**Planning Horizon:** 2026-2027

---

## Roadmap Overview

### Strategic Themes

**2026:** Establish Product-Market Fit & Scale
* Q1: Launch MVP and validate core value proposition
* Q2: Prove sustained engagement and refine experience
* Q3: Scale user base and launch multi-pet features
* Q4: Build platform and ecosystem integrations

**2027:** Platform Leadership & International Expansion
* Become the standard for pet emotional intelligence
* Expand internationally (UK, Canada, Australia, EU)
* Launch B2B partnerships (pet services, insurance, research)
* Introduce AI-powered predictions and proactive insights

## 2026 Q1: MVP Launch (January - March)

**Theme:** Successful Product Launch & Early Traction

### Goals
* Ship 15,000 devices to early customers

* Reach 3.5 DAT average

---

### January 2026: Pre-Launch Refinement

**Engineering**
- ✅ **Optimize battery life to 7+ days** (Currently 5.2 days)
  * Target: 7-day battery minimum
  * Optimize on-device ML inference
  * Reduce Bluetooth polling frequency
  * Status: In Progress

- ✅ **Reduce app crash rate to <0.5%** (Currently 1.2%)
  * Fix Bluetooth disconnection handling (42% of crashes)
  * Optimize translation history loading (28% of crashes)
  * Improve background notification processing (18%)
  * Status: In Progress

- ✅ **Improve "Dramatic Sighing" ML model to 75%+ accuracy** (Currently 64%)
  * Expand training dataset (500+ labeled examples)
  * Test multi-breed model variations
  * Add context signals (time of day, recent activity)
  * Status: In Progress

**Hardware**
- ✅ **Finalize production-ready collar design**
  * Reduce weight by 30% for cat comfort
  * Improve waterproofing (IP67 rating)
  * Lock in manufacturing specs
  * Status: In Progress

**Product**
- ✅ **Add "Do Not Disturb" scheduling feature**
  * User-configurable quiet hours
  * Smart batching during DND (deliver summary after)
  * Target: Reduce notification fatigue by 40%
  * Status: Planned

- ✅ **Implement basic multi-pet support**
  * 2-device household limit
  * Side-by-side drama comparison view
  * "Who's more dramatic?" widget
  * Status: Planned

**Operations**
- ✅ **Secure chip supply (Qualcomm QPM6100)**
  * Pre-order 10,000 chips with Q2 delivery
  * Establish dual-source backup (MediaTek MT6631)
  * Status: In Progress

- ✅ **Launch pre-order campaign**
  * Open pre-orders January 15
  * Target: 2,000 pre-orders (20% waitlist conversion)
  * Early bird pricing: $129 (vs. $149 regular)
  * Status: Planned

### April 2026: Retention Optimization

**Product**
- **Launch Drama History Timeline**
  * Infinite scroll of past dramatic moments
  * Filter by drama category, date range, Drama Index
  * Search functionality
  * User research priority #1

- **Weekly Drama Report Cards**
  * Automated summary every Monday
  * Top 3 most dramatic moments
  * Drama Index average and trend
  * Shareable Instagram/Twitter graphic
  * User research priority #3

- **Confidence scores for translations**
  * Show AI confidence (e.g., "92% confident")
  * "Why this translation?" explainer
  * Per-category accuracy stats in settings
  * Builds trust through transparency

**Engineering**
- **Notification optimization A/B testing**
  * Test timing strategies (immediate vs. batched)
  * Test frequency caps (max per day)
  * Test notification copy variations
  * Goal: Improve open rate from 52% to 65%

**Data Science**
- **Begin pattern insights development**
  * Identify recurring drama patterns
  * Time-based insights (e.g., "Most dramatic on Monday mornings")
  * Trigger-based insights (e.g., "Drama spikes when you eat pizza")
  * Roadmap for Q3 launch

## Roadmap Dependencies & Risks

### Critical Path Dependencies

**Launch Blocker (Q1):**
* ✅ Battery optimization to 7+ days (In Progress)
* ✅ Manufacturing capacity secured (10K+ units)
* ✅ Chip supply locked in (Qualcomm QPM6100)
* ⚠️ ML accuracy improvements (64% → 75% for Dramatic Sighing)

**Scale Blocker (Q3):**
* Infrastructure capacity (100K users)
* Manufacturing scale (MOQ: 50K units)
* Support team hiring (1:5,000 user ratio)

**Platform Blocker (Q4):**
* Alexa/Google certifications (8-10 week lead time)
* Partnership agreements signed
* API security audit completed

---

### Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|--------|------------|-------|
| **Chip shortage delays launch** | Critical | Dual-source strategy; pre-order buffer | COO |
| **ML accuracy doesn't improve** | High | Delay launch of low-accuracy categories; transparency | CTO |
| **Multi-pet adoption below 15%** | Medium | Enhance cross-pet features; discount bundles | CPO |
| **Smart home cert delays** | Medium | Launch API first; certifications follow | CTO |
| **Retail partnerships slow** | Low | Focus on DTC; retail is upside | COO |

## Roadmap Principles

### 1. **User Value First**
Every feature must deliver measurable user value (engagement, retention, delight)

### 2. **Ship, Learn, Iterate**
Launch MVPs quickly, gather data, refine based on real usage

### 3. **Platform Thinking**
Build for extensibility; every feature should enable future features

### 4. **Quality Over Speed**
Never compromise on ML accuracy or app stability for faster shipping

### 5. **Community-Driven**
Prioritize features requested by users; co-create with Drama Club

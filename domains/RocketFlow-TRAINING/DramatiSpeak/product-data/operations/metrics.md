# Current Metrics & Tracking: DramatiSpeak™

**Last Updated:** December 19, 2025

---

## Executive Summary

**Status:** Pre-Launch / Pilot Phase
* **Pilot Households:** 42 active testers across 3 cities (NYC, Austin, Portland)
* **Pilot Duration:** 6 weeks (Nov 4 - Dec 15, 2025)
* **Waitlist Size:** 2,300 signups
* **Pre-orders:** Not yet launched

**Key Highlights:**
* 🟢 Strong engagement: 3.2 DAT average (target: 4.5)
* 🟢 High early retention: 68% D7 retention
* 🟢 Excellent social sharing: 78% share rate (may be inflated due to early adopter bias)
* 🟡 ML accuracy: 78% overall (target: 80%+), with some categories below target
* 🟡 D30 data: Not yet available (pilot too new)

## North Star Metric

### Daily Active Translations (DAT)

**Current:** 3.2 translations per device per day
**Target:** 4.5 translations per device per day
**Trend:** ↗️ Improving (+0.4 over past 2 weeks)

#### Breakdown by Species
| Species | DAT | Sample Size |
|---------|-----|-------------|
| Cats | 3.8 | 28 devices |
| Dogs | 2.4 | 14 devices |

**Insight:** Cats generate significantly more dramatic moments than dogs. May need dog-specific model improvements.

#### Breakdown by Drama Category
| Category | Avg Daily Occurrences | % of Total |
|----------|----------------------|------------|
| Judgmental Cat Mode | 1.2 | 37% |
| Fake Hunger Alert | 0.9 | 28% |
| Attention Deficit Theater | 0.6 | 19% |
| Dramatic Sighing | 0.3 | 9% |
| Existential Crisis | 0.2 | 6% |

**Insight:** "Judgmental Cat Mode" and "Fake Hunger" dominate. Need to expand dog-specific categories.

## Pilot Study Metrics

### Engagement Metrics

#### Daily Active Users (DAU)
* **Current:** 85% of pilot devices (36/42)
* **Trend:** Steady over 6 weeks
* **Benchmark:** Excellent for pilot phase

#### Translation Notification Open Rate
* **Current:** 52%
* **Target:** 70%
* **Trend:** Declining from 71% (Week 1) to 52% (Week 6)

**Insight:** ⚠️ Notification fatigue setting in. Need to test timing optimization and content variety.

#### Weekly App Opens per User
* **Current:** 14.2 opens/week (avg 2 per day)
* **Trend:** Declining from 21 opens/week (Week 1) to 14 opens/week (Week 6)

**Insight:** Users check app less frequently over time, but still engage 2x daily. Consider proactive engagement strategies.

---

### Retention Metrics

#### D7 Retention
* **Current:** 68% (29/42 users active on Day 7)
* **Target:** 75%
* **Benchmark:** Good for new product, but below target

#### D14 Retention
* **Current:** 62% (26/42 users)
* **Trend:** 6 percentage point drop from D7

#### Weekly Cohort Retention
| Week | Active Users | Retention Rate |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| Week 1 | 42 | 100% (baseline) |
| Week 2 | 38 | 90% |
| Week 3 | 35 | 83% |
| Week 4 | 32 | 76% |
| Week 5 | 29 | 69% |
| Week 6 | 29 | 69% |

**Insight:** Retention stabilizing around 70% after Week 4. D30 data needed to validate long-term engagement.

#### Churn Reasons (Exit Survey)
* **44%** - Pet won't tolerate collar device
* **22%** - Novelty wore off faster than expected
* **17%** - Translations not accurate enough
* **17%** - Other technical issues (battery, connectivity)

**Actions Needed:**
* Design lighter, more comfortable collar
* Improve drama category diversity to prevent boredom
* Enhance ML accuracy (especially dramatic sighing)
* Fix battery drain issue (currently 5 days, target 7+ days)

### Product Quality Metrics

#### ML Model Accuracy (User-Validated)

| Drama Category | Accuracy | Sample Size | Status |
|----------------|----------|-------------|--------|
| Judgmental Cat Mode | 92% | 340 events | ✅ Exceeds target |
| Fake Hunger Alert | 78% | 267 events | 🟡 At target |
| Attention Deficit Theater | 81% | 178 events | ✅ Exceeds target |
| Dramatic Sighing | 64% | 89 events | 🔴 Below target |
| Existential Crisis | 74% | 52 events | 🟡 Near target |
| **Overall Average** | **78%** | **926 events** | 🟡 **Near target** |

**User Feedback Method:** Thumbs up/down after each translation

**Insight:** Need targeted improvement on "Dramatic Sighing" detection. Likely due to:
* Subtle audio signals (hard to detect)
* High variation across breeds
* Context-dependent (sighing can be genuine fatigue)

## Hardware Performance Metrics

### Device Reliability
* **Defect Rate:** 7% (3/42 devices had hardware issues)
  * Bluetooth module failure: 2 devices
  * Battery drain issue: 1 device
* **Target:** <3% defect rate for production
* **Status:** 🔴 Above target - manufacturing improvements needed

### Battery Life
* **Current:** 5.2 days average per charge
* **Target:** 7+ days
* **Status:** 🔴 Below target

**Usage Breakdown:**
* Translation processing: 42% of battery
* Bluetooth connectivity: 31%
* Background monitoring: 22%
* Other: 5%

**Improvement Plan:** Optimize on-device ML inference, reduce Bluetooth polling frequency

### Device Comfort (Pet Acceptance)
* **Pets Wearing Comfortably:** 79% (33/42 pets)
* **Pets Refusing Device:** 14% (6/42 pets)
* **Pets Tolerating (w/ resistance):** 7% (3/42 pets)

**Species Breakdown:**
* Cats: 75% acceptance (21/28)
* Dogs: 86% acceptance (12/14)

**Insight:** Need lighter collar design for cats. Dogs tolerate device better.

## Key Learnings & Action Items

### What's Working ✅
1. **Strong engagement from target users** - Urban millennial cat owners love the product
2. **High social sharing** - Viral potential validated
3. **Core concept resonates** - "Drama translation" is immediately understandable and entertaining
4. **Judgmental Cat Mode accuracy** - 92% accuracy proves ML approach works

### What Needs Improvement 🔧
1. **Battery life** - 5.2 days vs. 7+ day target
2. **App crashes** - 1.2% crash rate too high
3. **Dramatic Sighing accuracy** - Only 64%, needs model improvement
4. **Device comfort for cats** - 21% rejection rate concerning
5. **Notification fatigue** - Open rate declining from 71% to 52%

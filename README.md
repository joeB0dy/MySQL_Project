# MySQL Goals — Internship Capstone Project

## SMART Goal

**By August 4th**, I will complete a capstone project that demonstrates my technical skills in Zork workflow, transform codes, SQL queries, AI-assisted documentation, and application support by delivering at least one working solution that simplifies workflows and improves decision-making for my team.

To accomplish this, I'll identify a specific business problem that can be solved using the technical skills I've developed during my internship, then build a solution that combines SQL data queries with AI tools to automate processes or provide better insights. I'll document my work thoroughly to show what I learned and how the solution can be maintained, then present my final project to my team and manager to demonstrate the value I've added during my time here.

---

## How to Accomplish This Goal

### Weeks 1–2: Choose Your Project Focus
Talk with your manager and team members to identify a problem that aligns with what you've learned about Zork workflow, transform codes, and SQL queries. Look for opportunities where your technical skills can make a real difference. Maybe it's automating a report that currently takes hours to generate manually, or creating documentation that uses AI to explain complex transform codes. Pick something that showcases multiple skills you've developed during your internship.

### Weeks 3–4: Build the Core Solution
Start developing your project using the technical skills you've gained. Write SQL queries to extract and organize the data your solution needs. Apply your knowledge of Zork workflow and transform codes to ensure your solution fits into existing processes. If you're using AI for documentation or automation, integrate those tools in a way that's practical and useful. Focus on creating something that works, even if it's not perfect yet.

### Weeks 5–6: Test and Refine
Share your work with team members who would actually use it. Get their feedback on whether it solves the problem you identified. Test your SQL queries to make sure they're returning accurate data. Verify that your understanding of transform codes and workflow is correct. Use this time to fix bugs, improve performance, and make your solution more user-friendly based on real feedback.

### Weeks 7–8: Document and Present
Create thorough documentation that explains what your project does, how it works, and how others can use or maintain it. This is where you can showcase your understanding of AI-assisted documentation techniques. Prepare a presentation that walks through the problem you solved, the technical skills you applied, and the impact your solution has on the team. Include examples of your SQL queries, explain how you worked with transform codes, and demonstrate your understanding of application support by showing how your solution can be maintained going forward.

---

## Demonstrate Your Learning

Throughout the project, keep notes on what you've learned about each technical area. When you present your final project, you'll be able to speak confidently about how you:

- Applied **Zork workflow** principles
- Wrote complex **SQL queries**
- Worked with **transform codes**
- Used **AI tools** for documentation
- Thought about ongoing **application support**

This capstone project is your chance to show that you didn't just learn these skills in theory — you can actually apply them to solve real business problems.
